Welcome to Tired Travellers

A website offering tips and advice about where to go and what to see in Europe

Those of us at Tired Travellers have spent a lot of time travelling in Europe, and not just to the Louvre and the British Museum, though they are both phenomenal and worth visiting. You can find that kind of advice almost anywhere. We're here to offer suggestions and recommendations for places to visit, tours that are worth taking, and cafes and restaurants that won't disappoint and are slightly off th beaten track.
